What We Do

Our Services

We cover every aspect of house clearance — quickly, cleanly, and with full compliance.

Full House Clearance

Complete end-to-end clearance of any property — from studio flats to large family homes. We remove furniture, appliances, clothing, and general waste efficiently and responsibly.

Partial Clearance

Only need specific rooms or items cleared? We offer flexible partial clearances tailored to your exact needs — no unnecessary charges for work you don't need.

Post-Clearance Cleaning

Once cleared, we can leave the property spotless. Our post-clearance clean covers all rooms and surfaces — please note oven cleaning is not included in this service.

Probate & Estate Clearance

We understand the sensitivity required when clearing a loved one's home. Our team handles every item with respect and discretion throughout the entire process.

Landlord & Rental Clearance

Tenants left belongings behind? We'll clear the property swiftly so you can get back to letting. Fast turnaround with responsible disposal of all items.

Garden & Garage Clearance

Sheds, garages, outbuildings, and gardens piled up with years of items — we clear the lot. All waste is sorted, recycled, or donated where possible.

Simple Process

How It Works

Three straightforward steps from enquiry to a cleared, clean property.

1

Get in Touch

Fill in our free quote form or give us a ring. Tell us the number of bedrooms and the type of clearance you need — we'll get back to you promptly with a no-obligation estimate.

2

Property Assessment

We'll visit the property at a time that suits you, assess the volume of items, and confirm a fixed price. No hidden costs, no nasty surprises on the day.

3

We Clear & Clean

Our team arrives on the agreed date and gets the job done efficiently. We handle all disposal, recycling, and donating — and can clean up after if required.

Our Story

A Family Business Built on Trust

We're a family-run house clearance company based in New Alresford, Hampshire. We started Witchard because we saw how stressful property clearances could be — whether it's downsizing, dealing with a bereavement, or just clearing a lifetime of belongings.

We treat every property as if it were our own — with respect, efficiency, and a genuine care for the people we're helping. As a family team, we're hands-on at every job. You'll always deal with us directly, never a subcontractor.

We're fully licensed waste carriers, CRB checked, and carry full public liability insurance — so you can trust the job will be done properly and legally, every time.
